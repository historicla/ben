Selling Your Home

Selling is a home is an incredibly delicate process that requires trust and care. I understand this not only as a realtor, but as a property owner, as I have bought and sold many homes of my own.

In today's market, simply putting a "for sale" sign in a front yard and listing a property for sale on the MLS isn't good enough. An agent must be able to reach buyers through creative avenues such as cutting edge social media marketing, professional networks and unique property presentations that make a home stand out above the rest.
