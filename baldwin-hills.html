Baldwin Hills

Baldwin Hills / Baldwin Vista & Baldwin Village

Just a couple of miles South of Culver City are a series of lush, rolling hills in between La Cienega Blvd & Labrea Blvd, which make up the Baldwin Hills (The Dons), Baldwin Vista & Baldwin Village. These homes offer some of the most picture perfect northeast facing views in the entire City. They are younger neighborhoods, primarily constructed in the 1950s & 1960s, and are full of lovely minimal traditional and Mid Century Modern architecture.

Like View Park, these neighborhoods are on the doorstep of many new developments, and in the last several years the neighborhood has seen an influx of young families and children, many first time home buyers, who love the quiet streets and close proximity to Culver City, Hollywood and several freeways.
